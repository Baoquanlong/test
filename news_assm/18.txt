General public budget revenue in the first 5 months increased by 24.2% year-on-year
　　 Beijing, June 17 (Reporter Qu Zhehan) The Ministry of Finance announced on the 17th the fiscal revenue and expenditure data for the first five months of this year. In the first five months of this year, the national general public budget revenue was 9645.4 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 24.2% and a year-on-year increase of 7.3%. From a single-month data point of view, in May, the national general public budget revenue increased by 18.7% year-on-year, an increase of 6.8% over the same period in 2019.　　 From January to May, the central general public budget revenue was 4573.5 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 27%; local general public budget revenue at this level was 5071.9 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 21.7%. National tax revenue increased by 25.5% year-on-year, of which domestic value-added tax increased by 24% year-on-year, corporate income tax increased by 21.8% year-on-year, domestic consumption tax increased by 16.6% year-on-year, personal income tax increased by 25.9% year-on-year, and import tax revenue increased by 28.7% year-on-year. Non-tax revenue was 1262.3 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 16.2%.　　 From January to May, the national general public budget expenditure was 9355.3 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 3.6%. Among them, the central general public budget expenditure at this level was 1.2 trillion yuan, a year-on-year decrease of 8.9%; the local general public budget expenditure was 8155.3 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 5.8%. Expenditures in key areas such as education, social security and employment are relatively rapid.　　 In addition, in terms of government fund budget revenue and expenditure, in the first five months, the national government fund budget revenue was 2,970.4 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 25.6%; the national government fund budget expenditure was 2,961.4 billion yuan, a year-on-year decrease of 7%.
