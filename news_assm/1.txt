                            Over the past 20 years, the SCO has established a model of a new type of international relations featuring win-win cooperation
        This year marks the 20th anniversary of the establishment of the Shanghai Cooperation Organization. In the past 20 years, following the "Shanghai Spirit" of mutual trust, mutual benefit, equality, consultation, respect for diverse civilizations, and seeking common development, the SCO has always maintained a healthy and stable development momentum and successfully explored a new type of regional organization's cooperation and development path. It has established a model of a new type of international relations featuring mutual respect, fairness, justice, and win-win cooperation, and has made positive contributions to promoting the building of a community with a shared future for mankind.
