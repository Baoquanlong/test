The Ministry of Public Security held a press conference on June 17 People's Daily Online, Beijing, June 17th. The Ministry of Public Security will hold a regular press conference at 15:30 on June 17, 2021 (Thursday) in Beijing Rui'an Hotel to inform the national public security organs of the effectiveness of the measures taken to control telecommunication network fraud.  Liu Zhongyi, Director of the Criminal Investigation Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security, Jiang Guoli, Deputy Director of the Criminal Investigation Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security, Shi Yijun, Chief of Criminal Investigation Team of Shanghai Public Security Bureau, Wang Huojian, Deputy Chief of Criminal Investigation Team of Zhejiang Provincial Public Security Department, and Li Guozhong, Spokesperson of the Ministry of Public Security, attended and responded to reporters. ask.
